My views on LIFE

What do you think about life?

Honestly, I don't think of life as a gift from God or something. I think of it as more of a forced situation you have to live in because your parents decided to have a kid they could yield their desires from and continue their generation, and unfortunately, that kid was you. Like wtf, you are unwillingly brought to this good for nothing world and your path is already framed, and even if its not, you can't do anything about it. You have to go to school, you have to give exams, you have to obtain good marks and you have to do these even if you don't want it in the first place. Why?Because everyone does that. I wonder how this all started? Like if you are living life you are supposed to enjoy that, aren't you? I don't enjoy school. You are sent to school at a very young age; At that age you don't know what is good for you, and when you get enough conscience ,you realize that you are walking on an unescapable path.

Existential Crisis

Have you ever had existential crisis?

To put it simply existential crisis means like 'When you think of this whole universe (like how huge it is), you feel that your existence is worth nothing. It is common to have existential crisis and I don't think there is anything wrong to it. Well just think of this earth, how many people live here. Who would think that he/she stands a chance? And yes, I also had existential crisis in the past but I got over it quickly. Suppose there is a lottery and the chance of winning it is 1 in a 1,000,000.You are a person among those 1 million people. But here is the twist; No matter how many people participate in the lottery, the is always a winner.Maybe you could be that winner. Death

What do you think of death?

Honestly, I think of death as a redemption and freedom from all responsibilities. I don't think its a bad thing. I don't understand why people hate death. Why would anyone want to miss a chance to escape from all responsibilities? And yes I'm not afraid of death. I am just afraid of the pain in process of attaining it. And again I don't understand why suicide is a crime. If one is brought to this world without his/her will, he/she shall at least have the right to leave it according to their will .And I don't think you need a serious or precise reason to end your life.

And yeah that's what my views about life are. I don't know if they are any different from any other person's views, but at least my views on life are firm and I guess I will live my life based on these.
